Pickles

Pickles often are hated or loved. Yet, many people have yet to try homemade pickles. Homemade pickles taste very different from the store bought kind. They are richer in flavor, less sweet, and less acidic. They go great with other foods, but are themselves a delicious treat. Below you will find how to make them yourself!

Ingredients Needed:

  • Fresh cucumbers
  • Vinegar
  • Spices(dill, cumin, etc.)

Steps:

  1. Place freshly picked cucumbers (do not refrigerate) into a mason jar.
  2. Pour pure Vinegar into jar till full.
  3. Swish spices into jar until evenly spread.
  4. Place in a refrigerator until cucumbers are no longer white.
