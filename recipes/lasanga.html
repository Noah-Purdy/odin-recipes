Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish featuring layers of wide pasta sheets, savory meat sauce with onions and garlic, a blend of cheeses including ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, and often a velvety bechamel sauce. Seasoned with herbs like oregano and basil, the layers are baked until the pasta is tender and the cheese is golden and bubbling. It's a hearty and flavorful comfort food, appreciated for its rich taste and satisfying texture.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets
  • Meat Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Bachemel Sauce
  • Herbs and Spices

Steps:

  1. Prepare meat sauce
  2. Prepage Bachemel sauce
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  4. Prepare cheeses
  5. Assemble Lasagna
  6. Add Bachemel sauce
  7. Bake and serve
